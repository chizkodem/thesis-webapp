Background

Transportation systems provide essential mobility options globally. However, they face significant challenges in ensuring passenger safety and emergency response due to restricted space, high passenger loads, and emerging risks.

Significance of the Study

This study aims to develop a comprehensive transportation monitoring system using IoT, improving safety and emergency response capabilities in public transportation systems.

Objectives

  • Enhance passenger safety through real-time monitoring and alerts.
  • Improve emergency response times and coordination.
  • Address the needs of priority passengers, including the elderly and people with disabilities.

Goals

The goal is to create a resilient and sustainable public transportation system by integrating advanced IoT technologies to address safety and emergency challenges effectively.

Methodology

Our research involves developing an IoT-enabled monitoring system that integrates various sensors and communication devices for real-time monitoring and response capabilities.

Approach

We will leverage IoT technologies to create an integrated system that detects environmental changes, triggers automatic alerts, and enhances communication and coordination among passengers, drivers, and emergency responders.

Expected Outcomes

  • Improved passenger safety and confidence in public transportation systems.
  • Enhanced emergency response times and efficiency.
  • Greater resilience and sustainability of public transportation infrastructure.
